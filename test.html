Drift Hunters

What is the Drift Hunters game?

Drift Hunters is a fun driving game that involves performing high-speed drifts in a variety of iconic cars. It features 10 exciting tracks where you can practice and perfect your drifting techniques to earn points, which can be used to upgrade your car or unlock new ones. With realistic physics, smooth controls and extensive customization options, the game showcases the sheer awesomeness of the drift.

How to Play

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move the car
  • Spacebar to handbrake
  • C to change camera position
  • Left Shift to shift up gears
  • Left Control to shift down gears
